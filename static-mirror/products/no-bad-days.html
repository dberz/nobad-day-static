    Our story

    I'm David, the founder of No Bad Days. Born and and raised in New York City, I was surrounded by a culture of nightlife, alcohol, and excess. In my 20s, I began to learn more about health and wellness. I got into peak physical shape, married a doctor, and led the product and technology teams at several consumer health companies focusing on functional medicine, psychedelic medicine, sleep health, and musculoskeletal health.

    As I became more health-conscious, I started to worry about the short- and long-term effects of alcohol and cannabis. Slightly later, as I became more self-conscious (through meditation, mindfulness, men's work, and therapeutic psychedelic practices) I expanded my focus to the health impacts of psychedelic medicines like ketamine, MDMA, and psilocybin.

    The Birth of No Bad Days

    Combining my healthcare experience and interests with the expert advice of my physician wife and others, I decided to create No Bad Days. Our goal is to help people recover, restore, and protect their health while using social substances.

    Our Mission

    No Bad Days aims to grow health and self-consciousness, supporting the transition from social substance use to safer, therapeutic use. We want our product to help you enjoy your experiences without compromising your long-term health.

    Join us on this journey to better health!

    -David, Founder, No Bad Days
